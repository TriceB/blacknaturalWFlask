About the Creator

My name is Mon'Trice Brightmon. I created this website as way to support and patronize Black Owned Businesses across different industries and make it easier for others in my life to do the same.

I created and coded this as a learning opportunity and personal project as I am currently a Student of Unlock Academy, learning how to code.

Disclaimer: I do not own any of the photos shown on this website. All photos were sourced from the respective brand's website or the brand's Social Media page (Instagram or Facebook).

For all of the Personal Care (Skin, Hair, etc) and Food/Drink Brands included, I did my best to research the ingredients used to ensure they were natural/organic and do not include any harmful chemicals. Please be sure to do your own personal research on any ingredients to ensure you do not have any allergies or sensitivities.
