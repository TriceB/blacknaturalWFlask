About the Creator

My name is Mon'Trice Brightmon. I created this website as way to support Black Owned Businesses and make it easier for others in my life to do the same. For all of the Personal Care (Skin, Hair, etc)and Food/Drink Brands included, I did my best to research the ingredients used to ensure they were natural/organic and do not include any harmful chemicals. This was created and coded by myself as learning opportunity and personal project as I am currently a Student of Unlock Academy, learning how to code.
